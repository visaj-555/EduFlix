About Our Website

"EduFLIX" is a web-app where the user can view or access various study materials like Reference Books, Letho, PDFs, Previous Question Papers, Paper Solutions of various educational institutes ranging over whole state i.e. like selective institutes. The users can view, download, save i.e. keep a track of his/her necessary and relevant books, materials, resources, question paper solutions etc. so that he/she can later review the materials saved/bookmarked.
